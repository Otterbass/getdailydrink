“This is a demo web app providing general information on hydration and dietary intake. While it is generally recommended that women drink around 2.7 liters and men 3.7 liters of total water daily, hydration needs can vary based on factors such as climate, physical activity level, weight, and individual health conditions. This app does not offer medical advice. Caffeinated beverages, foods, and other factors also contribute to hydration, and maintaining a balance of salt and potassium intake is essential for overall health. Please consult a healthcare professional before making any changes to your hydration or dietary routine.”
